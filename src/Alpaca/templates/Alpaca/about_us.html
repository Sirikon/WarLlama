{% trans "What is Alpaca?" %}

{% trans "Alpaca is an activity management system application. That is, a place where you can create and manage your activities. The home page serves for a public index of all activities in Alpaca. This way, anyone can see your activity and join in! And you may join others' activities too!" %}

{% trans "Why 'Alpaca'?" %}

{% trans "We have spent quite a lot of time enjoying INERS Cultural Association's activities and, eventually, getting involved with it and helping it grow. One day, we both just realized we needed something that would allow us to organize and manage activities without the need of messy chat groups. Somewhere where not only the activity could be easily managed —who is coming? who is not?— but also somewhere where potential attendants could see all of our available activities." %}

{% trans "And we wanted it to be available to anyone and everyone in need of a similar system. Furthermore, make it a way of advertising the activities at our localities, reaching out to any groups and people wanting to make something happen, and giving them the chance of fair, equal advertisement for their activities." %}

{% trans "So we reached each other, having thought separately the same. Our ideas merged and... Alpaca was born." %}
